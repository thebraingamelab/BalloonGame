Your goal is to discover a rule that determines which combinations of liquids will react together.

To do this, use the 6 containers on the side to add liquids to the flask, and then press the MIX! button to test them.

You can press the "undo" button to remove the last added liquid or the "empty flask" button to clear all added liquids.

You will also be given an intial hint in the form of a combination of liquids that is guarenteed to produce a reaction.

Once 4 minutes are up you will be presented with a set of 16 combinations which you will need to classify. You can skip to this phase early by pressing the "I've figured it out" button if you're confident you've figured out the rule sooner.

Testing a combination of liquids will advance a turn counter. This has no effect on scoring and may be removed in a future version. Take as many turns as you need to figure out the rule.
